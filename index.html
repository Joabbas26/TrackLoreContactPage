TrackLore

Identify music from anime, movies, and TV shows.

Privacy Policy

TrackLore respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the TrackLore app.

Information Collection

TrackLore does not collect personally identifiable information. Audio used for music identification is processed only to identify songs and is not stored.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple ShazamKit and music service links (Apple Music, Spotify). These services may handle data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Detected songs may be saved locally on your device for history purposes. TrackLore does not store this data on external servers.
