Privacy Policy

TrackLore respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the TrackLore app.

Information TrackLore Processes

TrackLore listens to audio for the purpose of identifying music. Audio is used only to detect and identify a track and is not stored by TrackLore.

Data Stored on Your Device

TrackLore may save identified songs locally on your device so you can view your history. This history is stored on your device and is not uploaded by TrackLore.

Third-Party Services

TrackLore may use Apple technologies such as ShazamKit and may provide outbound links to music services (for example, Apple Music or Spotify). These services may process data according to their own policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
